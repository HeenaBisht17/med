Women in Medical Science

Honoring the brilliance, dedication, and discoveries of women who have transformed medicine—from laboratory research to frontline care.

Dr. Anandibai Joshi

  • Known As: First Indian woman to become a doctor of Western medicine.
  • Born: 31 March 1865, Maharashtra, India
  • Medical Degree: Graduated from the Woman’s Medical College of Pennsylvania, USA, in 1886.
  • Contribution: Pioneered women's medical education in India. Became a symbol of courage and defiance in a male-dominated society. Her success inspired generations of Indian women to pursue medicine.
